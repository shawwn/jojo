* naming

*** in c++

    - do not use `camelCase`, use `snake_case` instead.

    - use `_t` for type, `_p` for predicate

*** in jojo

    - do not use `camelCase`, use `lisp-case` instead.

    - use `-t` for type, `-p` for predicate
